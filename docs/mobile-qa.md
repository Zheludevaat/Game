# Mobile QA Matrix

## Devices

- iPhone SE size, Safari, portrait and landscape.
- iPhone 14/15 class size, Safari, portrait and landscape.
- iPhone PWA installed from Safari.
- iPad Safari landscape.
- iPad Safari split-view narrow width.

## Required Checks

- Menu fits without scrolling.
- Archetype select fits without scrolling.
- Character choice cards are tappable and readable.
- Gameplay landscape shows all doors.
- HUD does not cover doors or player.
- Floating left joystick begins where the thumb lands on the left half.
- Joystick does not steal taps from right-side buttons.
- Attack, cast, cycle weapon, cycle spell, map, and pause are reachable.
- Bottom safe area is clear of the iOS home indicator.
- Rotation does not leave a blank canvas.
- Audio unlock works after first tap.
- Music does not crackle after five minutes.
